How to Play

Click "Start Song" to begin the alphabet song simulation, or type the letters on your keyboard!

  • Follow along with the song and learn your ABCs!
  • Use Pause to stop the song temporarily
  • Use Reset to start over from the beginning
  • Type letters on your keyboard to highlight them
  • Toggle fullscreen mode for an immersive experience
  • Hide/show controls using the button in the top left
